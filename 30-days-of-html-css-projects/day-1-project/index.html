Selena Gomez

Born: 22 July 1992 (age 32 years), Grand Prairie, Texas, US

Singer, Actor, Fashion Designer, Music Artist, Voice Actor, Dancer

Selena Gomez is an American singer, actress, and businesswoman. Born in Texas in 1992, she began her career as a child actress, starring on the children's television series Barney & Friends. She rose to fame as a teen idol for her lead role in the Disney Channel sitcom Wizards of Waverly Place. As a recording artist, she formed the pop rock band Selena Gomez & the Scene and has since released several successful solo albums. Gomez has also ventured into producing and business, launching her own clothing line and serving as executive producer on the Netflix series Thirteen Reasons Why.

Selena Gomez is known for her philanthropic work and is a vocal advocate for mental health awareness. She has openly discussed her experiences with anxiety, depression, and bipolar disorder, using her platform to inspire others and destigmatize mental health issues. Gomez is also a dedicated advocate for lupus, a chronic autoimmune disease she was diagnosed with in 2015. She has founded the Rare Beauty brand, which aims to promote self-acceptance and mental health awareness through its products and initiatives.
